Generic Trivial Source Character Tables of \(PSL_2(q)\) and \(SL_2(q)\)


  • [BFL22] B. Böhmler, N. Farrell and C. Lassueur, Trivial source character tables of \(SL_2(q)\), Part I,
    J. Algebra 598 (2022), 308-350. [doi | arXiv | Oberwolfach Preprint]
    contains the following cases:
    • q odd, \(\ell\) odd, \(\ell\mid (q\pm 1)\)
    • q odd, \(\ell=2\), \( q\equiv\pm 3\pmod{8}\)
  • [FL23] N. Farrell and C. Lassueur, Trivial source character tables of \(SL_2(q)\), Part II,
    To appear in Proc. Edinburgh Math. Soc. [arXiv]
    contains the following cases:
    • q even, \(\ell\mid (q\pm 1)\)
  • Open: defining characteristic
Here \(\ell\) denotes the characteristic of the field over which we do representation theory, not the defining characteristic of the groups.
